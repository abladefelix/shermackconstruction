Who we are

Shermack Construction is a based in the North-West of England. We specialise in the designing, building, financing, operation and the management of infrastructure projects in the United Kingdom and Africa. We aim to provide opportunities that protect our environment and improve people’s lives through efficient and modern construction technologies and programs.

Shermack Construction is a company that has its prime focus on the assemblage of expertise in the execution of building structures, the market of property and the management of facilities in commercial, private and public sectors. With an experienced team dedicated to achieving high standards of clients, Shermack Construction is committed to making sure all services are executed swiftly and with maximum effort.

Why Choose us

We know construction is about milestones and deadlines and we excel at staying ahead of problems. From our clients to our employees, we understand that a good foundation comes from trust and skilled execution. In today’s world of construction, the latest technology is key to be a leader in the industry. Shermack construction utilises resources such as Building Information Modeling (BIM), PlanGrid and field staff iPads amongst others. We utilise lean construction techniques and project delivery management principles that create a collaborative, aligned team to improve the efficiencies and outcome of our construction projects.
