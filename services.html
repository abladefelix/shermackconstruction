Construction

Shermack Construction dedicates time to a pre-construction process, which is why it adopts the Integrated Project Delivery (IPD) which is essential to the success of an entire construction project. During this phase our range of services help our clients in understanding the entire scope and layout of the projects with respect to plan, schedule, potential issues and solutions, cost, design and more importantly laying out the goals and objectives of clients.

Our experienced Project Managers supervise the smooth and seamless transition of each phase in the entire project. Shermack has a collaborative team built of professionals who create a feasible and effective working strategy with the goals and objectives of the client as high consideration and make it a point to leave no stone unturned in the execution of every given project.

Shermack Construction is committed to achieving projects of high value through ensuring quality work while delivering on the client’s vision.

Facility and property management
